Privacy Policy

I wrote this myself — no legal copy-paste. Your financial life is private, and I want you to know exactly how PesoNest treats it.

📅 Last updated: May 2026 🇵🇭 Made for Filipinos

🙋 A quick note from me

I'm Wilfredo, the solo developer behind PesoNest. I built this app because I personally needed a simple way to track my finances in pesos — and I couldn't find one that felt right.

One thing I was very intentional about from the start: I didn't want to build yet another app that hoovers up your personal data. Your transactions, your loans, your savings — that's your business, not mine. PesoNest was designed with that in mind from day one.

📱 Your data lives on your phone

Everything you enter into PesoNest — your transactions, cards, loans, savings goals, budget limits — is stored locally on your device using SQLite. That's a database that lives entirely on your phone, not on a server somewhere.

I don't have a dashboard where I can look up your spending. I genuinely cannot see your data. That's by design.

No account required. No email. No sign-up form. You install the app and it just works.

☁️ The two times something goes online

There are exactly two features that involve a server, and both of them only happen when you deliberately choose to use them.

Joint Loans — If you share a loan with someone via QR code, PesoNest temporarily stores the loan data on a server so both phones can stay in sync. It's linked to a random anonymous ID that gets assigned to your install — not your name, not your number, nothing personal. I can't tell who it belongs to even if I tried.

Device Transfer — Moving to a new phone? This feature puts a temporary encrypted copy of your data on the server for up to 24 hours. Once your new phone picks it up, it's deleted. The code is one-time use and expires on its own.

That's it. Those are the only two moments data leaves your device, and both require you to actively initiate them.

🚫 What I don't collect — at all

To be completely straightforward:

  • I don't collect your name, email, or phone number
  • I don't store real bank account or card numbers — what you enter in PesoNest is just a label for your own tracking
  • I don't track how you use the app, what screens you visit, or how long you stay
  • No analytics. No ads. No selling data to third parties.

I'm not running an ad business on the side. PesoNest makes money through Premium subscriptions, not your information.

🔔 Notifications

PesoNest can remind you when a loan payment is due, or nudge you on a savings schedule you set. These are all scheduled on your device — no server involved, no internet needed for them to fire.

You're in full control of this. Turn them on or off anytime from the Settings screen or your phone's own notification settings.

💎 If you go Premium

Premium is handled by Apple or Google — whoever's app store you downloaded PesoNest from. I use RevenueCat to verify subscription status, but I never see your billing details. Your card info stays between you and Apple or Google.

📤 Exporting your data

Premium members can export a full backup as a JSON file, or pull just the transactions as a CSV. When you do this, the file gets created on your phone and shared directly through iOS or Android's share sheet — your email, Google Drive, AirDrop, whatever you choose. It doesn't pass through any server on the way out.

🛡️ Security

For the features that do touch a server, everything goes over HTTPS. On the backend (Supabase), I've set up Row Level Security — which means a device can only ever access data that belongs to it. Your loan data is invisible to every other device, and theirs is invisible to you.

🔄 If I ever change this

If I ever make a meaningful change to how PesoNest handles data, I'll tell you through the app before it happens. I won't just quietly update this page and move on.
