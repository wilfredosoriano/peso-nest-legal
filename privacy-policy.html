Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version

PesoNest was built with one thing in mind: your financial data stays yours. I didn't want to build another app that quietly collects everything you do. Your transactions, loans, and savings are your business, not mine.

Your data lives on your phone

Everything you enter into PesoNest (transactions, cards, loans, savings goals, budget limits) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. That's a database that lives entirely on your phone, not on a server somewhere.

I don't have a dashboard where I can look up your spending. I genuinely cannot see your data. That's by design.

No account required. No email. No sign-up form. You install the app and it just works.

The two times something goes online

There are exactly two features that involve a server, and both only happen when you deliberately choose to use them.

Joint Loans: If you share a loan with someone via QR code, PesoNest temporarily stores the loan data on a server so both phones can stay in sync. It's linked to a random anonymous ID, not your name or number, nothing personal.

Device Transfer: Moving to a new phone? This feature puts a temporary encrypted copy of your data on the server for up to 24 hours. Once your new phone picks it up, it's deleted. The code is one-time use and expires on its own.

What I don't collect — at all

  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Real bank account or card numbers
  • How you use the app (no analytics, no tracking)
  • Your location, contacts, or photos
  • Advertising identifiers

I'm not running an ad business on the side. PesoNest makes money through Premium subscriptions, not your information.

Notifications

Loan due date reminders and auto-save nudges are all scheduled on your device. No server involved, no internet needed for them to fire. You can turn them off anytime from Settings.

Premium purchases

Premium is a one-time purchase handled by Apple or Google. I use RevenueCat to verify your purchase, but I never see your billing details. Your card info stays between you and the App Store.

Exporting your data

Premium members can export a full JSON backup or a CSV of transactions. The file is created on your phone and shared through iOS or Android's share sheet. It doesn't pass through any server on the way out.

Security

Any time data touches the server, it goes over HTTPS. I've set up Row Level Security on the backend so a device can only access data that belongs to it.

If I ever change this

If I make a meaningful change to how PesoNest handles data, I'll tell you through the app before it happens. I won't just quietly update this page and move on.
