01 Agreeing to these terms

By downloading and using PesoNest, you're agreeing to these terms. I kept them short on purpose. No one should have to read a 20-page document just to use a budgeting app.

02 What PesoNest is, and isn't

PesoNest is a personal finance tracker. You enter your transactions, cards, and loans manually, and the app helps you see where your money is going.

It's not a bank. It's not connected to any financial institution. Think of it like a digital notebook for your finances. I built the notebook, you fill it in.

03 Your data, your responsibility

I can't verify whether what you enter is accurate. If you log the wrong amount, PesoNest will show the wrong picture. It has no way to know otherwise.

  • Double-check your entries, especially for loans
  • Keep your phone locked. Anyone who picks it up can open the app.
  • Only share loan QR codes with someone you actually trust
  • Back up your data regularly using the export feature in Settings

04 Not financial advice

PesoNest can show you how much you've been spending, but it can't tell you whether you should take out a loan or how to invest your savings. It's a tracking tool, not a financial advisor. For decisions that actually matter, talk to someone qualified.

05 Premium

Some features require a Premium upgrade, which is a one-time purchase through Apple or Google. Once you buy it, it's yours. No renewals, no recurring charges. Refunds go through Apple or Google's standard policies.

06 Joint Loans

When you generate a QR code, PesoNest stores the loan data on a server temporarily. Once the other person scans it, you're linked. They'll see updates when you make a payment, and vice versa.

There's no way to unlink after someone claims the code, so be sure about who you're sharing it with. The QR code is single-use.

07 Device Transfer

It generates a 6-character code that holds your encrypted data on a server for 24 hours. Your new phone enters the code, pulls the data, and the server copy is deleted. Don't share that code with anyone during the window.

09 If something goes wrong

I built PesoNest to be reliable, but I'm one person and phones are unpredictable. If your device gets wiped or the app crashes, I can't recover data that only existed on your phone.

Please export a backup from time to time. Settings → Export Data → JSON. It takes less than a minute and could save you from losing months of records.

10 Deleting the app

Uninstalling PesoNest removes everything stored locally. There's no cloud backup I can restore for you. Export your data first if you think you might want it later.
